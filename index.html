Work

      Web design / illustration

      • Dragon Curve

      Dragon Curve is entrepreneurship network for young leaders. I redesigned their site using the red from their logo. I created the mission statement icon and the various patterns used throughout the mockup.

    • Finance Tracking App

      October 2014

      App Design / Data Visualization

      • Finance Tracking App

      The personal finance tracking app was a vision I had to make budgeting more fun and beautiful. I wanted bright and vibrant colors to personally help distract me from financial planning. Additionally I created all of the svg graphs and charts. It's designed so the first thing you notice are upcoming bills for the month, but then it seamlessly transitions into budgeting goals.

    • ManoType

      September 2014

      web design / illustration

      • ManoType

      ManoType is a program created to help people learn to type and reach their individual typing goals. The site is designed to be minimalistic since the intended use is for long periods of typing. The darker color scheme is used so that it is slightly easier on the eyes. In addition to designing the sites look and feel I also created the svg hands, keyboard, and geometric start page.

    • Kiki Riki

      September 2014

      webdesign / development

      • Kiki Riki

      KikiRiki is a turkish fashion company that is opening a new store in NYC and needed a USA website. I designed the layout and then developed a template using bootstrap for them to add/change the content with their internal team. I used bold black lines to compliment the KikiRiki logo, while not distracting from the images in their lookbook.

    • Green City Mockup

      March 2014

      Web Design / Logo Design

      • Green City Mockup

      The mockup of a website's first page. Using just black and white with a splash of color, the mockup stays minimalistic. The top picture of a cityscape is overlaid with patterns to create a stain glass look. This visual is intended to give the mockup a level of detail and class.

    • Energy Presentation

      January 2013

      Presentation design / Illustration

      • Energy Presentation

      Created as a mock-up presentation for an energy company. The prospective client was looking for slides that could capture the look and feel of clean, sustainable energy. Coming into a new and exciting market with many possibilities, I chose bright, vibrant colors. I overlaid graphs and information on high resolution pictures, a tribute to the classic idea of sustainability: reuse.

    • Data Visualization

    • Asteroid Mining

      July 2012

      Presentation design / illustration

      • Asteroid Mining

      A presentation about mining in space. Crafted to educate about the future possibilities of mining asteroids for resources. Youthful colors are intended to give a perception of playfulness, while the detailed asteroids add a level of realism. The intended result is visual conversation juxtaposing reality with possibility.

    • Gongora Wine

      March 2010

      Packaging design / illustration

      • Gongora

      A concept for the label of an innovative start up winery, Gongora. The project consisted of displaying modern art on a wine bottle. Greatly influenced by the Russian abstractionist, Kandinsky, the label is transparent with gradient overlays. As the wine is consumed, light shines through the label morphing the look and feel of the wine bottle. It is quite literally "the art of consumption".
