  • ManoType

  • Gongora Wine

    March 2010

    Packaging Design / Illustration

    • Gongora

    A concept for the label of an innovative start up winery, Gongora. The project consisted of displaying modern art on a wine bottle. Greatly influenced by the Russian abstractionist Kandinsky, the label is transparent with gradient overlays. As the wine is consumed, light shines through the label, morphing the look and feel of the wine bottle. It is quite literally "the art of consumption".
