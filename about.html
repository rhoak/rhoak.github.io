The bit about myself

Like many designers in the digital space, the work had a way of finding me. A passion that started with a box of crayons and legos eventually found momentum in storytelling.

With a background in both visual design and front-end development, I design digital products that integrate visual communication and functionality. I design clean interfaces, create elegant experiences and tell compelling stories.

Understanding the problem and the person is always at the core of my work. I consider myself one part researcher and one part creator, using observation and research to help craft and cultivate designs. I consider creativity a process— one that flourishes in a room with many diverse ideas and plenty of iteration.
